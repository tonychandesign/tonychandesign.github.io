Helpful Nudges
Customers weren’t using CPA services despite opting in for it. The hypothesis was that by reminding users that they have unlimited access to live help at personalized points of need, we could reach users during moments of uncertainty, resulting in increased usage, reduced FUD, and increased satisfaction with the product. This resulted in a +1.3% conversion (~$9.8M).
