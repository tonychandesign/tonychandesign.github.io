This track of work includes my marketing and in-product motion projects. The animations are either created with production grade JS/CSS code or After Effects with AirBnB’s Lottie library. The medium I use is determined by the technical constraints of the team I work with. I had a content and visual partner for all of these projects.

Helpful Nudges
Customers weren’t using CPA services despite opting in for it. The hypothesis was that by reminding users that they have unlimited access to live help at personalized points of need we can reach users during moments of uncertainty, resulting in increased usage, reduced FUD, and increased satisfaction with the product. This resulted in a +1.3% conversion (~$9.8M).
